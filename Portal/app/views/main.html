"We get fibre built to you"

  • The demand for fast, reliable fibre access has skyrocketed. There are many companies providing fibre, but they will only build to your home when enough demand is created in your area.

  • The Problem

    So why does it feel like fibre companies are not listening to you? Why are they not building fibre to you? Are you not important enough? Are you not living in an area “deserving” enough? The answer is that each fibre company builds based on demand. They have a web site (or mutliple partner web sites) where you can register your interest. When there is enough interest, your area "qualifies" and the process starts. However, because there are so many different companies that your neighbourhood can log interest with, there is no single view from any of these companies of the TRUE demand. So even if your area truly qualifies, the numbers are so spread across multiple websites, databases and companies that no single fibre build company will know.

  • The Solution: FibreCentral.co.za

    The solution is very simple – there needs to be a central place where everybody logs their interest for fibre. The fibre build companies then need to have access to this data and they need to use it to understand just how many people want fibre and where! This is where we come into the picture. At FibreCentral.co.za we have created a central, unbiased, unaffiliated place for you to register you interest in getting connected. As you, your neighbours, friends and families register interest we will manage on your behalf the fibre companies to show them where to build.

    We also plan to offer you more - we want to help you pick a service provider (ISP) and a home device (CPE) to connect to the web. We will do this by enabling you to compare packages, prices, service and quality for all of these. In short, we want to be THE central place that you can come to for fibre broadband connectivity!

  • Important info

    We are NOT AFFILIATED to any of the fibre companies. We will speak to ANY company that want to take your collective interest for fibre, but we are NEUTRAL and will always be neutral as we have your best interest at heart. We are also not a fibre network build company, service provider or CPE provider company.

    Remember to tell your friends, family, colleagues and neighbours to visit our site and show their interest in getting fibre. The faster we get the interest, the faster we can get the message to the people who build and the faster you will get connected!

    Note: The service we provide is free to you.
